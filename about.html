About Us

Two generations, one standard

GWT Hardwood Floors is a family business — started by a husband and wife, carried on by their two sons, and held to the same standard it was built on more than twenty years ago.

Where it started

Gary & Mary Turner, early 2000s

The business was founded in the early 2000s by Gary and Mary Turner, who built their reputation on craftsmanship, honesty, and high-quality hardwood work throughout Pontiac and the Metro Detroit area.

Gary handled the technical side of the craft — sanding, refinishing, installation, and custom stain work. Mary managed operations and customer relationships. Together they created a company known for reliability, attention to detail, and floors that stand the test of time. Their commitment to quality still guides the business today.

The next generation

Jake & Greg, learning at their father's side

Gary and Mary's two sons, Jake (40) and Greg (37), have been part of the business since graduating high school in 2004 and 2008. Both grew up learning the trade directly from their father — and over the years have become an even stronger, more refined version of him when it comes to installing and refinishing hardwood floors.

Today the family brings decades of combined experience, pride, and dedication to every home — delivering floors that look beautiful, wear well, and make homeowners proud of their space.
