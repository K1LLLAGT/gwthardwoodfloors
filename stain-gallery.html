Stain Catalog

The Duraseal colors we work from.

We finish almost every floor with Duraseal — a deep, reliable line we know inside and out. The full catalog is below to get your eye going. The same stain looks different on every species, grain, and room's light, so we always prove your final color on a sample board milled from your own floor.

Why custom?

The same stain, two different floors

Wood is a living material. A color that reads warm on white oak can turn pink on red oak; a gray that looks perfect in showroom light can go cold in a north-facing room. That's why we treat a color chart as a starting point, never a final answer.

We blend from the full Duraseal line and prove the result on sample boards built from your actual floor — next to your cabinets, your trim, and your daylight. Some of our best colors, like our signature WheatStone Oak, are two Duraseal stains mixed by hand.

Signature blend
WheatStone Oak
Duraseal Nutmeg + Country White, mixed 1:1 · Pallmann Ultra Matte topcoat

A desaturated light brown with gray, green-neutralizing, and soft pink-oak undertones — equally at home in warm or cool interiors.
