Stain Gallery

Your color, mixed by hand

Stain isn't a number on a can — it's a starting point. We blend colors and prove them on sample boards milled from your own floor, because the same stain looks different on every species, every grain, and every room's light.

Why custom?

The same stain, two different floors

Wood is a living material. A color that reads warm on white oak can turn pink on red oak; a gray that looks perfect in showroom light can go cold in a north-facing room. That's why we don't sell color from a chart.

We build sample boards from your actual floor, blend stains in front of you, and adjust until it's right — next to your cabinets, your trim, and your daylight. The result is a color that belongs in your home.
