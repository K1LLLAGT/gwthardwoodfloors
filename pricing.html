What affects the price

Where your quote comes from

Six things move a flooring quote up or down. Knowing them helps you understand exactly what you're paying for.

01

Square footage

The biggest single factor. Larger continuous areas are usually more efficient per square foot than several small, broken-up rooms.

02

Condition of the floor

Deep scratches, water damage, pet stains, cupping, or failed prior finishes take more prep before color even goes down.

03

Stain complexity

A single off-the-shelf color is straightforward. Custom blends, bleaching, and cabinet-matching mean sample boards and extra time.

04

Finish system

Water-, oil-, and alcohol-based systems differ in product cost, number of coats, and dry time, which affects both price and schedule.

05

Stairs

Staircases are detailed, hand-worked surfaces with many edges. They're priced separately from open floor area.

06

Repairs & extras

Board replacement, transitions, trim, and moving large or built-in items add scope to the job.
